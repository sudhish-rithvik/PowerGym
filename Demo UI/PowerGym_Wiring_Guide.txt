
POWERGYM EQUIPMENT MONITORING - WIRING DIAGRAM
=============================================

ESP32 DEVELOPMENT BOARD CONNECTIONS:
===================================

SPEED SENSORS (IR Optical Sensors):
- Treadmill Speed Sensor:
  * VCC → 3.3V (ESP32)
  * GND → GND (ESP32)  
  * OUT → GPIO 2 (Digital Input)

- Stationary Bike Speed Sensor:
  * VCC → 3.3V (ESP32)
  * GND → GND (ESP32)
  * OUT → GPIO 4 (Digital Input)

- Elliptical Speed Sensor:
  * VCC → 3.3V (ESP32)
  * GND → GND (ESP32)
  * OUT → GPIO 5 (Digital Input)

- Rowing Machine Speed Sensor:
  * VCC → 3.3V (ESP32)
  * GND → GND (ESP32)
  * OUT → GPIO 18 (Digital Input)

WEIGHT/FORCE MEASUREMENT (Load Cell + HX711):
============================================
- Load Cell Red Wire → E+ (HX711)
- Load Cell Black Wire → E- (HX711)
- Load Cell White Wire → A- (HX711)
- Load Cell Green Wire → A+ (HX711)

- HX711 VDD → 3.3V (ESP32)
- HX711 GND → GND (ESP32)
- HX711 DT → GPIO 19 (ESP32)
- HX711 SCK → GPIO 21 (ESP32)

POWER MEASUREMENT CIRCUIT:
=========================

VOLTAGE SENSOR (Voltage Divider):
- Generator Positive → R1 (10kΩ) → GPIO A0 (ESP32)
- GPIO A0 → R2 (2kΩ) → GND
- Voltage Range: 0-48V → 0-3.3V (ESP32 ADC)

CURRENT SENSOR (ACS712):
- Generator Positive → ACS712 IP+ Terminal
- Load Positive → ACS712 IP- Terminal  
- ACS712 VCC → 5V (External Supply)
- ACS712 GND → GND (ESP32)
- ACS712 OUT → GPIO A1 (ESP32)

STATUS INDICATORS:
=================
- Status LED (Green):
  * GPIO 23 → 220Ω Resistor → LED Anode
  * LED Cathode → GND

- Power LED (Red):
  * GPIO 22 → 220Ω Resistor → LED Anode  
  * LED Cathode → GND

POWER SUPPLY:
============
- ESP32: 5V USB or External 7-12V → VIN
- Sensors: 3.3V from ESP32 onboard regulator
- ACS712: External 5V supply required
- Load Cell: Powered through HX711 (3.3V)

EQUIPMENT INTEGRATION POINTS:
============================

TREADMILL:
- Speed Sensor: Mount on belt roller with reflective tape
- Power Tap: Generator connected to drive motor circuit

STATIONARY BIKE:  
- Speed Sensor: Attach to flywheel or crank arm
- Power Tap: Generator connected to resistance mechanism

ELLIPTICAL:
- Speed Sensor: Position on main drive wheel
- Power Tap: Generator integrated with stride mechanism

ROWING MACHINE:
- Speed Sensor: Mount on chain/cable pulley system
- Power Tap: Generator connected to resistance flywheel

GENERATOR CONNECTION EXAMPLE:
============================
Equipment Motor → Mechanical Coupling → DC Generator
DC Generator Output → Power Measurement Circuit → Battery/Load

Typical Generator Specs:
- Voltage: 12-48V DC
- Current: 1-20A  
- Power: 50-500W (depending on equipment)
